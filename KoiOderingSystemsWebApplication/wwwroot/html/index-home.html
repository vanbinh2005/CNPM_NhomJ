Latest News Updated July 16, 2024


◇Shipping fees during summer◇

Due to a significant increase in the price of styrofoam boxes, we will be increasing shipping fees by 500 yen for areas with delivery the day after the next day for orders placed on or after July 1, 2024.

This offer applies to orders placed between July 1st and September 30th for deliveries to Hokkaido, Chugoku region, Shikoku region, Kyushu region, and Okinawa Prefecture.

If you wish to ship outside the above areas in a styrofoam box, an additional 500 yen will be charged.

We appreciate your understanding as this is necessary to ensure the safe transportation of the Nishikigoi.

◇For customers ordering from our company for the first time◇

If you are purchasing from us for the first time, cash on delivery is not available.

We appreciate your understanding as we have been experiencing a number of cases of first-time customers refusing to pay by cash on delivery or being unable to deliver due to entering fictitious addresses.

Cash on delivery is also available for second and subsequent orders.

The system allows you to select this option, but once we confirm your order, we will change it to bank transfer and send you a confirmation email.

◇Notice◇ Alfish (oxygen generator) price increase

Due to the recent significant rise in prices, we will be increasing the price of Alfish (oxygen generator) to 1,200 yen per unit starting from orders placed on January 1, 2024 .

We appreciate your understanding.

We want as many visitors as possible to have a leisurely look around, so if you would like to visit, please contact us by phone or email.

◇About oxygen generators◇

Due to concerns about oxygen deficiency in the Nishikigoi during transport, we may ask customers ordering large Nishikigoi (mainly over 61 cm) and customers living in areas where the product will arrive two days or later to include an oxygen generator called "Alfish" (1,200 yen/box) in the same package.

We kindly ask for your understanding.
