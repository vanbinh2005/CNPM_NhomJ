Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Frequently asked questions and their answers are listed here.


If you have any questions that are not listed here, please feel free to ask.

1. I am an individual koi enthusiast and would be willing to make a small purchase?

Thank you, I'll be happy to assist you.
However, shipping costs are the same regardless of the quantity, so it may seem a bit expensive in terms of costs other than the price.
If you understand that, then we have no problem with it.
Please feel free to contact us.

2. I'm a beginner who is just starting out, so could you teach me how to raise koi from the beginning?

At Matsuda Koi Farm, we strive to provide high-quality koi with our limited staff.
We may not have time to answer questions or concerns from complete beginners.
Of course, we will be happy to answer any questions you may have regarding your purchase, but we are only targeting people who have enough experience and knowledge in carp rearing to be able to receive and release carp into breeding tanks at their own risk.
We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your understanding.

3. If I make an inquiry, do I have to purchase it?

We will endeavor to answer customer inquiries as quickly and clearly as possible, but if after reading our reply you are not satisfied for any reason, such as disagreement with the price or concerns, please feel free to refrain from purchasing.

Please make sure you are fully satisfied before purchasing carp, mackerel, etc.
There is no charge for simply making an inquiry.
However, if you decide not to purchase, please let us know.
Additionally, we strictly prohibit inquiries for the purposes of price research, browsing, etc.
(If the required information such as your name and address is not included, we may not respond to inquiries based on false information.)
Any personal information received from customers in response to inquiries will be handled with the utmost care.
Please check the Personal Information Handling page for information on how we handle personal information.
Click here for the personal information handling page

4. If I want to purchase one, how do I do it?

Please fill in all the necessary information on the order form, email, or fax to place your order.
After receiving your email or fax, we will check our inventory and then inform you of the total amount including the price, consumption tax, and shipping fee.
(Within 2-3 business days)
If you wish to pay by bank transfer, please transfer the amount we have notified you.
(Please let us know after you have completed the remittance procedure.)
Once we have confirmed your payment, we will immediately begin preparing for shipment.
As a rule, we aim to ship within four business days, but if there is a delay due to factors such as the condition of the carp or traffic conditions, we will be sure to contact you.
Please wait for the carp to arrive.
Please see the delivery procedure for more details.

5. How can I pay for the service?

Please pay by cash on delivery, bank transfer or cash in advance.
Please note that any bank transfer fees will be borne by the customer.

6. My koi arrived dead. What should I do?

Please contact us immediately. (TEL: 0258-37-3722)
We will take great care in preparing the shipment, but in the unlikely event that the koi is dead upon arrival, we will exchange it for an equivalent koi only if it is deformed.
*You will need to send us images that show death or deformity.
For details, please see the page on the Specified Commercial Transactions Act: Handling Returns and Defective Products.
Click here for the page regarding specific commercial transactions

7.Do you only sell the koi listed on your website?

If you have any requests or are looking for a specific carp, not just one-year-old fish, please contact us. We will provide you with the information to meet your needs.
Even if we don't have the carp you want right away, we will contact you as soon as it arrives.
Just like the question "If I make an inquiry, do I have to buy it?", an inquiry does not mean an order will be placed immediately, and no fees will be charged, so please rest assured.
Please take a look at the information about our koi farm before considering purchasing.
